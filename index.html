About

I'm Blanche, a creative technologist with a passion for design and a knack for solving complex problems. Over the past five years, I've worked on everything from repairing electronics to building web applications and interactive experiences.

In 2019, I participated in the TechNova Hackathon and won 2nd place in the Advanced category, which was a major milestone for me. I have experience in Python, JavaScript, and Linux administration, and enjoy learning new technologies.

I'm currently 23 years old and working as a Product Specialist for Lumina Motors, a company leading the way in sustainable transportation. My goal is to continue growing as a developer and designer, and to contribute to innovative projects that make a difference.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my background, see my portfolio, and get in touch to discuss how I can help with your next project. Thanks for stopping by!
